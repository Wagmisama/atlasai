General Questions

What exactly is an AI agent?

An AI agent is a software program that can perform tasks autonomously, make decisions, and interact with users or other systems. Our AI agents are specialized for business applications, capable of handling complex workflows, customer interactions, data analysis, and more.

How is AtlasAi different from other AI services?

AtlasAi focuses on creating specialized, task-specific agents rather than general-purpose AI. Our agents are designed to integrate seamlessly with your existing systems, learn from interactions, and provide measurable business value from day one.

What industries do you serve?

We work with clients across various industries including finance, healthcare, e-commerce, education, and more. Our AI agents can be customized to meet the specific needs of virtually any sector.

Technical Questions

What kind of technical expertise do I need to use your AI agents?

Our platform is designed to be accessible to both technical and non-technical users. For basic deployments, no coding is required. For advanced customizations, our team can assist or your technical staff can work directly with our well-documented APIs.

How do you ensure data privacy and security?

We implement enterprise-grade security measures including end-to-end encryption, strict access controls, and regular security audits. For sensitive data, we offer on-premise deployment options and comply with all major data protection regulations.

Can the AI agent integrate with our existing systems?

Yes, our AI agents can integrate with most business systems including CRMs, ERPs, databases, and APIs. We provide standard connectors for popular platforms and can develop custom integrations as needed.

Pricing & Support

Is there a free trial available?

All plans include documentation and deployment assistance. Optional ongoing maintenance ($49+/month) extends support coverage.
